Event s battery parametrami
- checkbox na Block other events
  - ak bude zakrtnuty, nespustia sa ziadne eventy, kim takyto battery event bezi
  - ak skonci bez - okamzite znovuspustenie eventov aka retart eventov
- ine ako battery event
  - pribudne checkobx - block by battery event
    - ak bude zaskrtnute, bude blokovatelny battery eventom
